Student Investment Fund

About Us

The Imperial Business School Student Investment Fund was established in 2018 to give students the opportunity to gain real-world experience in buy-side investments, equipping them with the essential practical skills needed to secure a role in this sector.

Run by an executive committee of current students, supported by both faculty and alumni of Imperial Business School, the Fund manages £100,000 of assets.

The Fund is structured into several specialised teams, each contributing to its overall success

Portfolio Management Team

Allocates capital across the Fund’s strategies to optimise risk-adjusted returns.

Quantative Team

Develops data-driven trading models using statistical and algorithmic techniques.

Fundemental Team

Analyses company fundamentals and macroeconomic trends to identify investment opportunities.

ESG Team

Focuses on ESG factors and sustainability-driven strategies.

Infrastructure Team

Maintains the Fund’s tech stack, software, and data systems critical for analysis and execution.

Marketing Team

Manages internal and external communications, events, and brand visibility.
